The Black Panther

Black panthers are known for their exceptional night vision, which is six times better than that of humans.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera
  • Average Lifespan: 12 - 15 Years
  • Average Length: 1.8 to 2.1 meters
  • Habitat: Tropical and deciduous forests

The term "black panther" typically refers to a melanistic color variant of leopards (Panthera pardus) or jaguars (Panthera onca), where their normally spotted or rosetted coat appears almost entirely black due to an excess of dark pigment melanin.
